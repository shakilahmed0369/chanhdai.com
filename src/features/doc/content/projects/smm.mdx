AwtoSMM is an AI-powered social media management and marketing SaaS platform designed to streamline content creation, scheduling, publishing, and performance tracking across multiple social media channels. It empowers businesses, agencies, and marketers to manage their entire social presence from a single unified dashboard.

Built with Laravel and React (Inertia.js), AwtoSMM delivers a fast, modern SPA-like experience. The platform leverages AI to generate content ideas, optimize posting schedules, analyze audience engagement, and provide actionable insights to maximize social media ROI.

## Core Features

- **AI Content Assistant** — Generate engaging social media posts, captions, and hashtags using AI-powered content creation tools
- **Multi-Platform Publishing** — Schedule and publish content across multiple social media channels from a single dashboard
- **Content Calendar** — Visual calendar for planning, organizing, and managing your social media content pipeline
- **Smart Scheduling** — AI-optimized posting times based on audience activity patterns and engagement data
- **Analytics & Reporting** — Comprehensive analytics dashboard with performance metrics, engagement tracking, and ROI measurement
- **Audience Insights** — Deep audience analysis with demographic breakdowns, behavior patterns, and growth trends
- **Team Collaboration** — Role-based access control for teams, agencies, and client management workflows
- **Media Library** — Centralized asset management for images, videos, and brand resources

## Admin & SaaS Features

- Built with Laravel 12 for modern architecture, stability, and scalability
- SaaS-ready with subscription-based business model and flexible plan management
- AI credit system with usage tracking, limits, and monetization options
- Advanced user management with granular role and permission system
- Built-in ticketing system, newsletter campaigns, and marketing tools
- Analytics dashboard with revenue trends, user growth, and platform usage insights
